Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about our healthcare solutions — SoftPulse HIS, MedPulse CMS, and AI-powered features designed for hospitals and clinics.

What is the difference between SoftPulse HIS and MedPulse CMS?

SoftPulse HIS is built for hospitals and includes modules like IPD, OPD, Pharmacy, Nursing, OT, Billing, and Inventory. MedPulse CMS is ideal for OPD clinics, offering features like e-Prescriptions, Appointment Booking, Visit History, and Doctor Dashboards.

How does SoftPulseAI integrate AI into hospital or clinic workflows?

We offer AI modules for automated prescription generation, nurse care planning, appointment no-show prediction, patient history reference, EMR automation, and document analysis. These can be added to our systems or integrated into third-party HIS/CMS platforms.

Is your HIS/CMS customizable for different departments and workflows?

Yes, both systems are modular and highly configurable. Whether it’s a small clinic or a multi-specialty hospital, we tailor the solution to match your exact operational needs, including department-specific workflows and forms.

Do you provide training and support for medical staff using your systems?

Absolutely. We provide onboarding, hands-on training, user manuals, and ongoing support for doctors, nurses, administrative staff, and billing teams to ensure smooth adoption and usage.

Can SoftPulse or MedPulse be integrated with laboratory, pharmacy, or billing systems?

Yes, integration is seamless. Our systems support HL7 and API-based integrations with lab equipment, pharmacy counters, billing gateways, and insurance platforms for end-to-end connectivity.

What kind of analytics and reports do your solutions provide?

Our HIS and CMS systems offer real-time dashboards, MIS reports, patient visit analytics, doctor performance insights, drug usage patterns, billing trends, and AI-powered predictions for hospital/clinic decision-making.
